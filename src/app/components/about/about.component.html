About

Daily fantasy lineups can be hard and time-consuming to make if you are trying to max enter a contest, or cover your bases for possible outcomes. Infinity Draft was developed to make the process of creating unique lineups easier by generating random lineups, biased towards creating lineups which contain players who score higher on average. Here are the steps we use to generate permutations:

  • 1 Process CSV file into an internal data format
  • 2 Sort players by their Average Fantasy Points Per Game
  • 3 Generate a random seed and pick a player from the list of players to add to the lineup. We can repeat this process up to 10,000 times per team in order to generate a unique lineup.
  • 4 We repeat step 3 until 1,000,000 random choices have been made
  • 5 We sort the resulting teams and display the ones with the highest score
